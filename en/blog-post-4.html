The Charm of Photography: A Beginner's Guide

August 20, 2025

My greatest hobby is photography. I'm fascinated by the idea of not just taking a picture, but also thinking about light and composition to capture a story in a single image. The golden hour at dusk is the best!

For those of you who want to start, I recommend beginning with your smartphone camera. Just by being conscious of composition and how you capture light, you can take surprisingly great photos. Finding the **"beauty in the everyday"** is what photography is all about.
