About My Hobbies

July 25, 2025

Hello! Today, I'd like to talk about my cherished hobby: **BVE Train Sim (BVE)**. I started this hobby because a friend of mine was playing it, and it looked interesting.

The appeal of BVE is that you can drive your favorite train lines on a PC. Every time I drive, it's always exciting!

If you're interested in getting started, I recommend checking out the BVE Wiki. I'm sure you'll discover something new.
