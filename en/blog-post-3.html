My Journey into Programming: Successes and Setbacks

August 20, 2025

My journey into programming began with a simple desire to create my own website. I didn't know anything at first and wasn't sure where to start.

The most difficult part was arranging the layout cleanly. The elements wouldn't align as I wanted, and I felt like giving up many times. But by referencing MDN Web Docs and YouTube tutorials, I gradually deepened my understanding. This website is the result of that learning journey.

To those of you just starting out, I recommend setting small goals first. And be sure to experience the **"joy of making things work."** It's the best motivator!
